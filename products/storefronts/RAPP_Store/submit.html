A rapplication is an agent plus a UI — bundled. Drop a .zip of your rapp directory, or point at a public GitHub repo. The receiver validates against SPEC.md and stages it for review.

Reference: SKILL.md (full guide for AIs & humans) · SPEC.md (validation rules) · repo

Sign in with GitHub to submit rapplications to the catalog.

Prefer a token? Paste a personal access token with public_repo scope. Stored in your browser only.

Submitting from a brainstem instead?
Drop @rapp/rapp_publish_agent.py into your brainstem. Hand it any path — it auto-detects whether it's an agent, a rapplication, or a sense, and routes to RAR, RAPP_Store, or RAPP_Sense_Store. One agent for the whole ecosystem. SKILL.md explains it to an AI.
