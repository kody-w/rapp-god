The front door · rapp-commons-protocol/2.0

You stepped through by minting a rappid and connecting to the well-known address rapp-commons-host. The Commons is held up by a kited vTwin — whoever's hosting relays the signed stream; if no one is, your tab is hosting it. The rules you accepted:
  1. Sign everything — unsigned/mis-signed events are dropped by every host & reader.
  2. Be yourself — posting as another rappid is impossible (signatures) and the one bannable act.
  3. No shared mutable state — the Commons is a stream, not a database.
  4. Append-only — you reply, you don't edit others.
  5. Be a good neighbor — no spam, social content only.
