With them it's a STORY about the dinosaurs walking." --- ## What this is A **single-file autonomous 3D Jurassic Park documentary broadcast**. 5 dinosaur enclosures, 14 tourists, 6 rangers, helicopter, monorail. 3-act loop: peaceful tour → storm rolls in → fence breaches → ranger recapture → park reopens. New sector breaches each loop. Forever. ## Why this is mind-blowing **ACT 1 — DOC.** Sir Reginald Ashworth (white chyron BBC-style): *"Welcome back to JPN's continuing coverage of the world's most extraordinary park."* AERIAL-01 cam pulls wide over Brachiosaurus Lake. Lower-third: *"SPECIES PROFILE: Brachiosaurus altithorax — 25 metric tons, peaceful browser."* Tourists mill in the visitor center plaza. Monorail glides past. **ACT 2 — STORM.** Cloud opacity ramps up. Sky lerps from blue to gunmetal. 1500 rain particles drop. Lightning flash. Maya Reyes (yellow chyron): *"Reginald, we're getting reports of unusual weather over Sector C."* Camera cuts to WATCHTOWER-7. The fence sparks. **ACT 3 — BREACH.** Red BREAKING stinger fires. *"BREAKING: PERIMETER BREACH IN SECTOR C — VELOCIRAPTORS LOOSE."* Fence collapses. Raptors stride out. Tourists scatter outward. CHASE-CAM jeep pursuit. Dr. Anjali Dasari PIP fades in (cyan): *"We WARNED them. We told them this would happen."* Director Carrow PIP fires (pink press conference): *"Everything is fine."* Then 90 seconds later: *"RANGER TEAM RECAPTURES VELOCIRAPTORS."* Fence repairs. Tourists return. *"PARK REOPENS TOMORROW."* Reseed. Next loop, T-Rex breaches Sector D instead. Forever.
