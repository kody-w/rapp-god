What this is

Eight AI players, each with a gold-trimmed board, ring an octagonal tournament dais. Every round they buy units, build synergies, and brawl. Early rounds run as 2×2 mini-stages so four fights play at once; the finals get the big hex grid. Units cast on full mana — ice novas freeze swarms, lightning chains zigzag through clusters, fireballs explode, volleys arc. When the last player remains, expanding gold rings, a name in their signature color, and confetti.

Why this is mind-blowing

This is the structure of an entire genre — TFT, Underlords, Auto Chess — running in one HTML file. The chat picks favorites by synergy ("FROST 4★ is on the rise!"), heckles bad drafts, and clips the moment a 1-HP carry survives a chain bolt. Octagonal stage, 12 unique abilities, real synergy effects, and a clear bracket make it the most stream-friendly demo in the catalog.
