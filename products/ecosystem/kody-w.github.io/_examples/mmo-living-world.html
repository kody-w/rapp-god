Sixty- four AI players walk, dance, cast, duel, emote, and join guild raids in a 3D town with an auction house, a mage tower, a tavern, a guild hall, and a dungeon portal. A multi-channel chat box scrolls in real time. World bosses spawn at -80z and a dozen players converge on them. Every nine minutes the shard restarts and the world repopulates with fresh names. ## Why this is mind-blowing It's not a fake MMO. It's a real server simulation. /general says "anyone need heals at portal", /guild says "RAID FORMING SE", /trade says "WTS Voidmaw shoulders 4500g", and three of the players actually start walking toward the portal. The director knows when the world boss spawns and pulls WORLD_BOSS_VIEW. A duel breaks out and the camera cuts to DUEL_CAM. The shard restart countdown runs at the bottom and the whole world goes black and rebuilds in front of you — like a real game day.
