Six named bakers compete in all three timelines simultaneously across five areas. Tent Main Hall is the long curved tent with six baker stations and the central proofing oven. Judge Table is the linen-draped sampling area with tasting forks and a tiered crystal stand. Proofing Room is the warm-yellow rising-dough room behind the tent. Garden Lawn is the sunlit hosts-banter area with bunting and a teapot. Award Stage is the white-gazebo crowning area with the silk ribbon. In Timeline A, Judge Whitford (recurring cast — yes, the same Whitford from R17 mall, R18 office, R18 cruise) is the head judge and he crowns the chocolate gateau. In Timeline B, Judge Mrs. Pemberton is head judge and she crowns the lemon-lavender tart. In Timeline C, Judge Doc Wendell Carrow is head judge and he crowns the rosemary brown-butter shortbread. Six contestants compete in ALL THREE timelines simultaneously — Mara Greenfell (Bramblestoke village), Olu Pemberton (mason's daughter, Stillwater Hold roots), Pip Sweetwater, Reggie Halberton, Vana Trill, and Bren Halloway each create three different bakes visible at once. The Timeline Fork Panel top-left shows the same focused baker across all three bakes (recipe / pan / step). Cordelia Brookline (recurring poet cast) and Detective Halloran (off-duty) host with gentle banter on the Garden Lawn. Nineteen director cameras cycle including dedicated splitscreen sweeps that show A/B/C side-by-side. The bake-off *remembers* — and so does the multiverse. localStorage saves under `bake_off_3paths_v1` (week, bakes shipped, triple crowns, kindness ledger). Crossover chyron reads `bramblestoke_brain_v1`, `meridian_cruise_v1`, `stillwater_hold_v1`, `nexlogic_returns_v1` and weaves THOSE events into bake-off lines.
