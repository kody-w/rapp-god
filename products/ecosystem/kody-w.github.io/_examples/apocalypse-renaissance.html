Civilization rises to its golden age, gets destroyed by one of 6 random catastrophes, survivors wander a wasteland, form tribes, build towns, and reach a new renaissance. 8 minutes per loop. 14 named characters thread the entire arc. Forever. ## Why this is mind-blowing **ACT I — COLLAPSE (Year 0).** Skyline cam wide. Gleaming city of glass towers. CHRONICLER VESPER (white avatar): *"This was humanity at its peak."* Then — randomly chosen this loop — an asteroid streaks down. Flash. Letterbox bars snap in. *"COLLAPSE: ASTEROID IMPACT — POPULATION 8.2B → 47M IN 6 HOURS."* Camera cuts to GROUND. Towers crumble. Screens go dark. **ACT II — WANDERING (Year ~5).** Cam: FIRE. Eight survivors around a campfire. Lower-third: *"WHO IS — MAREN, AGE 23, FORMER ARCHITECT."* HISTORIAN DOC YATES: *"What was once a city is now a memory. Maren remembers it."* Tint: cold blue. Grain heavy. **ACT III — REBUILDING (Year ~50).** Cam: NEW-TOWN. Wooden palisade walls. Granaries. Founders' meeting. Lower-third: *"WHO IS — MAREN III, GRANDDAUGHTER OF THE FOUNDER, TOWN ELDER."* Walls rise mesh-by-mesh. Banner: *"SETTLEMENT OF [TOWN] FOUNDED — POPULATION 312."* **ACT IV — RENAISSANCE (Year ~200).** Cam: GOLDEN-CITY. Plaza. Marble monuments. Painters at easels. Letterbox bars retract. Tint warm. Grain off. Banner: *"RENAISSANCE OF [CITY] — A NEW GOLDEN AGE."* CHRONICLER VESPER: *"And humanity rose again."* **ACT I — COLLAPSE (Year 0, NEXT LOOP).** This time it's plague. Or AI uprising. Or EMP. New characters. New founders. New renaissance city. Loop forever.
