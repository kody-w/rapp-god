What this is

FOUNDRY OS is a cinematic, offline startup reactor. Change one sentence or turn the variant dial and it deterministically forges a visible Brand Genome, honest domain candidates, three SVG marks, responsive landing and pricing pages, exactly fifty scored issues, a ten-slide markdown deck, and five editable fictional prospects with personalized outreach. Every bay is already alive when the demo opens, every asset is downloadable, and the same sentence plus variant always produces the same bytes.

Why this is mind-blowing

The impressive part is not eight isolated generators. It is one tiny brand constitution flowing through all eight: the same promise, vocabulary, voice, palette, motif, pricing unit, and fingerprint survive from logo geometry to backlog language to a cold DM. A built-in consistency scanner proves the alignment, while the entirely local demo stays honest about what it cannot know — domain candidates are never called available, and every prospect is explicitly fictional.
