Floor 2 (1968) is mid-century walnut shelves with a jazz radio playing — Cordelia Whitford runs the second floor as a vinyl-and-paperbacks reading lounge. The Timeline Fork Panel splitscreen auto-pairs the current floor to a peer floor (e.g., 1924 paired with 2087, mid-century paired with 1995). The bookstore *remembers*. localStorage saves `multilayer_books_v1` (cycle, drama meter, survivedTo year, twist fired) AND read-only loads TEN sibling demo keys for crossover chyron.
