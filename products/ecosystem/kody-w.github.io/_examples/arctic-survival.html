What this is

You're stranded on procedural snow plains. Body temperature gauge drops every second whether you act or not — it's the clock. Hunger drains slowly. Stamina depletes when you sprint. Approach a tree, E to chop — takes 3 chops, gives 1 firewood. Five firewood + open ground + E lights a fire that casts warm light and recovers temperature when you stand near it. Bow + 4 rabbits scattered for food (cooked at the fire). Three weather states cycle every 30-70 sec — CLEAR, OVERCAST, BLIZZARD. Blizzards cut visibility to 18m, double cold drain, and let wolves get close. At night, wolves spawn and orbit your fire — flee within 7m of a lit one, pursue if you wander further. Survive 5 nights to win.

Why this is mind-blowing

The Long Dark is a $25 game that took years to make and still gets patched. This nails its core loop in 1310 lines. The temperature gauge alone is the whole horror — you don't need the wolves, but you have them anyway.
