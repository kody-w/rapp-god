What this is

Eight cars in a dirt arena ringed by pre-baked bleachers full of silhouette spectators with rare colored shirts and the occasional camera flash. Each car has HP and an AI that hunts the weakest. Hits kick up tire tracks that accumulate across the floor. Wreckage glows orange while fresh. The drama-cam eases into big collisions. The bracket — quarters, semis, grand final — fills in live as winners advance. Last car running takes the cup, the field resets, and the next tournament begins.

Why this is mind-blowing

It's a complete sports broadcast in one tab. The bracket gives chat something to root for. The drama-cam makes every big hit feel like a clip. The tire tracks tell the story of who chased whom. The audio crunches sell the carnage. The grand final — usually two badly damaged survivors limping at each other — is genuinely tense. It loops forever with zero operator touch.
