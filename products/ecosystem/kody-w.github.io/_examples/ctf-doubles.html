What this is

Four named soldiers, two per team, on a symmetric open-canyon map: red base on the left, blue base on the right, a central rocky outcrop, and two side passes (open up top, cover-heavy at the bottom). Each team has a buggy with a driver and a gunner. Sniper rifle and rockets sit on the central rock; overshield is on the side passes. Flag carriers can't shoot — only melee + run — and drop the flag on death. Dropped flags return after 30 seconds. First to 3 captures or 8 minutes wins. MVP scorecard tracks caps, returns, kills, deaths, and vehicle kills, then auto-rematch.

Why this is mind-blowing

The matches play like real open-canyon CTF. Buggies splatter. Flag carriers panic. Test runs produced a 1-2 comeback with three captures at 51s, 151s, and 406s — including a flag-return defense and a buggy wreck on the killfeed. The roles flex on the fly: a bot will park the buggy, jump out to defend the flag, then hop back in for a counter-push. That's the difference between a game broadcast and a sim.
