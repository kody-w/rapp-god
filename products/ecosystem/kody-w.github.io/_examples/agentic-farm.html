Four farmhands and twenty-four named animals visibly tend the farm across five areas. The Barn is the warm-amber milking parlor with hay loft. The East Field is the noon-gold corn rows with the scarecrow at center. The Orchard is the dappled-green apple-and-pear grove with the picking ladder and three beehives buzzing in the dappled light. The Pond + Duck Corral is the blue-cool cattail-rim with weeping willow on the dock. The Farmhouse is the cream-soft front porch with the red mailbox and veg-garden raised beds. Cows Bessie, Daisy, Petunia, Clover, Hazel, Penelope, Buttercup, Marigold, Honey, and Willa. Chickens Henrietta, Chickpea, Nugget, Drumstick, Omelette, Yolkie, Frittata, and Coq. Sheep Cottonball, Snowdrop, Lambchop, and Mochi. Horses Ranger and Dapple. Each animal has a goal, a need, a plan, a current step, and a mood — all visible in the AGENTIC THINK PANEL with a species-icon variant. Bessie's plan reads "walk to willow → check shade → nap 4 minutes → ruminate" and you can watch her execute it. Plus four farmhands tending fence repairs, milking, egg collection, and orchard ladder work. Eighty ambient farm-tour visitors. Eighteen director cameras cycle. Pastel goal trails leave color-coded streaks (cow-beige, chicken-tan, sheep-cream, horse-mocha, farmhand-blue). Decision diamonds pop when an animal switches area. Interaction sparkles fire when animals get within 2.2m (cows greeting cows = soft pink puff). The farm *remembers*. localStorage saves the day counter, total Eggs collected, total Bushels picked, total Milk gallons, and a "Herd Ties" relationship graph (which cows hang together, which chickens patrol with which). Each ten-minute reseed advances the day, hatches a new chick, replants a row, and rolls fresh schedules. You don't play it. You watch it. And it remembers. ## Why this is mind-blowing The chyron is the secret weapon: "Day 23 — Bessie naps under the willow on cycle 4. Henrietta lays egg #147. Farmhand Tilly picks bushel #62 from the Honeycrisp tree." That single line stitches three areas in one beat — Bessie on the Pond willow-shade close camera with the soft pink ZZZ over her head (decision diamond as she drops to lying-down, plan-step ticks 3 of 4 "nap"), Henrietta on the Barn nesting-box close with egg #147 settling into straw (interaction sparkle as Frittata and Yolkie cluck approval, Egg counter ticks to 147), and Farmhand Tilly on the Orchard ladder camera with the Honeycrisp branch bouncing back (decision diamond as Tilly switches to next branch, Bushel counter ticks to 62). Multiply that by twenty-four animals each with a brain panel showing goal/need/have/plan/step/mood — including a chicken whose plan reads "patrol corn row → spot grub → claim grub → cluck victorious" — pastel goal trails species-coded, Egg+Bushel+Milk+Day counters compounding across reseeds (chick #1 hatches on cycle 5 then becomes Pebbles the named hen on cycle 6), a Herd Ties graph showing Bessie-Daisy hang together 47% of cycles, and a Stardew-cozy chyron beating Day×Act, and you've got a Babe-cozy stream that genuinely earns its agentic scope. ALL FICTIONAL — no real farms, no real farmers, no real chickens.
