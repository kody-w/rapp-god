Then the After the Final Rose reunion airs and a brand new season starts. ## Why this is mind-blowing Reality TV is a state machine, and this demo proves it. The 9-cam director knows when to zoom in on a confessional and when to pull wide to the helicopter taking off. Every season the lead changes, the archetypes reshuffle, and the show writes itself again.
