What this is

REDLINE TRIBUNAL is a cinematic, local-only code-review trial. A deterministic lexical engine indicts exact changed lines, then searches a fictional engineering history for the strongest token-shingle comparator. Every charge carries a rule, risk, rebuttal, citation, and reproducible fingerprint.

Why this is mind-blowing

The critic cannot hide behind confidence. Crimson lines lead back to the code you wrote; emerald lines lead to proof that you have written the same idea better. Turn the cruelty up or strip the theater away entirely—the findings never move. Your own ceiling becomes the standard, and every verdict remains auditable.
