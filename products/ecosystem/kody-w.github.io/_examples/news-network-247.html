Two anchors at the desk, four city correspondents on remote, a director cycling fourteen cameras, breaking-news wipes, a stock ticker that random-walks, six headline pools that template-fill with thirty fillers, a 4-way split, a commercial stinger, and a reseed every nine minutes that rebuilds the entire news cycle. ## Why this is mind-blowing It's GNN at 3am. The wall monitors behind the anchors actually show the same correspondent's face that's about to come on remote. The talking heads actually lip-sync. The director cuts to "WE GO LIVE TO LONDON" and a wipe takes you to Theo Hartwell in front of Big Ben. Then breaking news hits and the strobe banner overrides everything. Watch it for ten minutes and you've seen a full news cycle.
