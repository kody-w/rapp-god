The wedding is the payoff." --- ## What this is A **cozy 3D farm sim** that plays a full year on autopilot — Spring → Summer → Fall → Winter, 28 days each, 8 festivals, friendship building with 6 named villagers, and a wedding at year-end. Built around the village of **Pelican Cove**: farm, village square, beach, forest, cave, seaside cabin. ## Why this is mind-blowing The whole *Stardew Valley* loop is here, on autopilot, in 60 KB. The farmer wakes at the farmhouse (chimney smoke is a `Points` particle system). They water 12×10 tiles of crops — parsnip, cauliflower, blueberry, cranberry, ancient fruit — through 5 growth stages. They walk into Pelican Cove where Doc Penny is at the inn at 7am, Pierre opens the shop at 8, Marnie tends her ranch all afternoon. They fish off Elliott's pier. On Spring 13 it's the Egg Festival. On Spring 24 it's the Flower Dance. Friendship climbs from 0 to 10 with the favorite villager. By Winter 25 — Feast of Winter Star — friendship hits 10. They propose. **A wedding.** Year-end montage. New farmer. Roll the year. In the simulation logged above: ⚡ a Spring storm blessed a parsnip with lightning and turned it gold. Doc Penny accepted the proposal. 6,357g earned, 28 crops harvested, 15 fish caught, 17 festival visits. **And then it does it all again.**
