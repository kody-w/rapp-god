Banner: *"WAR ECONOMY — [CITY] BECOMES AN ARSENAL OF DEMOCRACY."* **1977 — BLACKOUT.** All windows go dark. Skybox brownout. CRIME stat bar spikes. Cam: HIGHWAY follows a car through the dim city. **1995 — TECH BOOM.** First 30-story tower rises in the southeast quadrant.
