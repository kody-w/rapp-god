What this is

Two champions, randomized from a 30-name roster, drop into a tight Quake-style arena with a central lava pit, two corner platforms, jump pads, a mega-health perch up top, and a quad-damage spawn on a 90-second timer. Best of five. Each round is first to 3 frags or 90 seconds. Bracket dots fill in live across the HUD. Rocket-jumps, bunny-hops, airshots — full Quake palette. Series winner gets a confetti-and-stats scorecard, then a fresh pair drops in.

Why this is mind-blowing

The two personalities make every match different. AGGRESSIVE rushes the lava pit edge and strafes for splash knockback. METHODICAL camps the mega perch with rail. When QUAD DAMAGE comes up at 90s, it's a rotation flashpoint — whoever gets it gets a 30-second window to close out a round. Bracket dots in the HUD turn a five-minute series into something with real shape and a real winner.
