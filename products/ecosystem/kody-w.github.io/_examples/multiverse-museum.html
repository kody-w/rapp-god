Exhibit 2 asks "What if Queen Audra had crossed the Whisperwood?" — the descendants founded Bramblestoke Village in one branch, which crosses over to `bramblestoke_brain_v1`. Exhibit 3 asks "What if the Inventor Pim had patented the cooling box?" — three branches show the cooling-box becoming a household appliance, a research-only artifact, or a state secret. Exhibit 4 asks "What if the Bramblestoke Treaty had been signed in summer not winter?" — three branches diverge. Exhibit 5 asks "What if the cat Smokey had not been adopted by the Court Astronomer?" — the most charming what-if.
