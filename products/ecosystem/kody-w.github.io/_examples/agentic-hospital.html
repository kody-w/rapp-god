The ER is on an urgent-orange-and-bright-white palette with a triage desk, four trauma bays, and an ambulance bay. The ICU is on a calm-blue palette with four beds, a monitor wall, and a family waiting area. Surgery is on a cool-green palette with three ORs, a scrub sink, pre-op, and post-op recovery. Pediatrics is on a sunny-yellow palette with kid-friendly rainbow murals, a toy box, pediatric beds, and a nurses' station. The Lab is on a clinical-aqua palette with a microscope station, blood draw, sample freezer, and an MRI shell. Corridor-pan transitions move the camera between wings every sixty to ninety seconds. Dr. Olivia Whitfield (Chief, rounds all wings), Dr. Marcus Tate (ER attending), Dr. Priya Banerjee (surgeon), Dr. Felix Hoffman (pediatrician), Dr. Sasha Kim (ICU), Nurse Manny Solano (ER), Nurse Ruby Park (peds), Nurse Bea Dawson (ICU), Lab Tech Owen Mears, and Janitor Mr. Chigozie (beloved by all, cleans every wing) each have a goal, a need, a plan, a current step, and a mood — all visible in the AGENTIC THINK PANEL. Mrs. Carmen Ortiz (broken arm, recovering), Tommy Park (age 8, sprained ankle, gets a sticker), Mr. Hauerwas (post-op recovery), and Mrs. Donovan (mild fever, ER → home) rotate through. The CHART-FLIP OVERLAY in the top-left ticks off Dr. Whitfield's pending charts as she rounds each wing. Goal trails on hallway floors show staff paths in scrub-color tints. Decision diamonds pop above staff on treatment decisions. Interaction sparkles + chart-update fire when staff consult each other. Nineteen director cameras cycle. Eighty instanced waiting-room civilians fill the wings. The hospital *remembers*. localStorage saves rounds completed, surgeries done, stickers given, lab samples processed, and consultation relationships between reseeds. Each ten-minute reseed advances the day; lifetime totals compound, Mr. Chigozie's clean-every-wing streak grows, Tommy Park earns more stickers. You don't play it. You watch it. And it remembers. ## Why this is mind-blowing The chyron is the secret weapon: "Day 4 Act 2 — Tommy Park gets a bravery sticker for his sprained ankle. Priya finishes Surgery #2 with a clean OR.
