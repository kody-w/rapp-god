Five station areas, each its own palette. Platform 7 is where the midnight train arrives. Station Cafe is warm-amber with a copper-topped counter and brass espresso machine. Ticket Hall is marble grandeur with brass dividers. Newsagent Kiosk is the compact news stand with the evening Tribune. Great Clock Mezzanine overlooks the concourse with three colored hands at different angular speeds — the gold hand for Timeline A (on time), the snow-blue hand for Timeline B (delayed), the copper hand for Timeline C (airship). In Timeline A the train arrives on time and all eight named passengers board normally. In Timeline B snow delays the train, passengers wait in the cafe, and unexpected friendships form over hot cocoa. In Timeline C the train is replaced by the ZEPHYR airship and passengers get an unexpected adventure overhead. Mrs. Pemberton (who somehow missed the SS Meridian — she's at Hallowell Junction tonight!), Mr. Whitford (returning from a NEXLOGIC trip), Inspector Halloran (off-duty, heading home after the Thornpike Job heist case), Cordelia Foxworth (heading to Bramblecrest Art School), plus four more named passengers complete the eight. 24 (8 × 3 timelines) named passengers + 3 staff (station-master, porter, barista) + 30 name pool + 80 ambient = 137 named souls. Snow flurries (420 flakes) drift across the platform in Timeline B. The locomotive (Timeline A) sits at the platform with three Pullmans. The Zephyr airship (Timeline C) hangs in the night sky. Nineteen director cameras cycle across all five areas. The station *remembers*. localStorage saves `paradox_station_v1` (cycle, drama ladders, twist fired) AND read-only loads SIX other demo keys (`meridian_cruise_v1`, `nexlogic_returns_v1`, `heist_crew_v1`, `eternal_brew_v1`, `schoolyard_v1`, `mall_of_dreams_v1`) for crossover chyron. Each ten-minute reseed runs a new midnight arrival. You don't play it. You watch it. And it remembers across six other demos. ## Why this is mind-blowing The chyron is the secret weapon: "11:47pm — Mrs. Pemberton in Timeline B realizes she missed the SS Meridian by 4 minutes today; the cafe regular at the next table is a face she remembers from the bookshop. That single line stitches three timelines AND three crossover demos in one beat — Mrs. Pemberton on the Cafe Timeline B close camera with the cocoa steaming and a fellow regular's nod (chyron pulls SS Meridian missed-by-4-minutes from `meridian_cruise_v1`, recurring cast pemberton +1, drama ladder rung 2 of 4 "missed cruise"), Whitford on the Platform Timeline A close camera with the Pullman door closing behind him (chyron pulls daughter's recital from `nexlogic_returns_v1`, drama ladder rung 4 of 4 "made the recital"), and Halloran on the Airship Timeline C close camera with the manifest catching the gold-trim light (decision diamond as Halloran clocks the briefcase, drama ladder rung 3 of 4 "spotted the briefcase"). The Timeline B cafe passengers turn — they recognize each other from OTHER demos. Mrs. Pemberton recognizes Cordelia from the bookshop. Halloran recognizes Whitford from NEXLOGIC.
