The overlord acts every 5 seconds — summoning monsters, placing trap glyphs, casting curses, taunting. The heroes A*-pathfind through the dungeon trying to reach the throne. 12 waves. One side wins. End screen. New overlord. New dungeon. Forever. ## Why this is mind-blowing The chronicle scrolls in three colors: `[VORTHAK]` purple for the overlord, `[HEROES]` gold for the party, `[CHRONICLE]` cyan for emergent events.
