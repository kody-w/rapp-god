What you're looking at

Powered by the public twin engine.

24 founder eggs were dropped into 4 biomes — forest, ocean, mountain, sky. Each biome rewards different traits. Migration costs fitness. Mating only happens within the same biome AND the same species. After 100 generations, geographic isolation has produced biome-specific dominant lineages — biogeography from first principles.
