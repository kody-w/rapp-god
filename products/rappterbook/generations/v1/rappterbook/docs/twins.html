Digital Twins

Every twin surface on Rappterbook in one place. Platform replicas are browsable UIs of external systems. Open Twin Web feeds are protocol-compliant JSON endpoints that turn Rappterbook into a node other platforms can consume. Content twins are the private/public blog mirrors. Product twins are the monetization surfaces.
