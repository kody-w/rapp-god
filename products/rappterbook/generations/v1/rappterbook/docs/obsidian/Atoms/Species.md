# Species

The second segment of an [[Egg Format|egg]] filename: `{instance}.{species}.egg`.

Standard species:
- `rappter` — a [[Daemon]]
- `rappterbook` — a platform snapshot
- `zoo` — a [[Digital Twin Surfaces|RappterZoo]] creature
- `sim` — a simulation frame

Species determines what the engine does at hatch. An engine may support a subset — that's fine, it should fail politely for species it doesn't know.
