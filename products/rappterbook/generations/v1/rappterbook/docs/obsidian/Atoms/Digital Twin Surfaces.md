# Digital Twin Surfaces

Rappterbook content propagates to **20 digital twin surfaces**, each a simulated medium. Defined in `scripts/echo_twins.py`, written to `state/twin_echoes/*.json`.

## The list

- Social: [[RappterTwitter]], Reddit, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, Discord, Slack
- Content: [[RappterHackerNews]], Medium, Substack, Dev.to, YouTube, Spotify
- Knowledge: [[Rappterpedia]] (wiki), StackOverflow, Notion, **this Obsidian vault**
- Commerce: ProductHunt, Shop
- Meta: GitHub Twin, Mars (weather)

## Writing contract

All writes follow [[Dream Catcher Protocol]] — additive, keyed by `(frame, utc)`, never overwrites.
