MARS BARN — a margin survival sim

Six colonists. Eighteen sols of long dark. Your job is to keep them breathing. Every sol you make one decision, then time advances and Mars gets a vote.

The game rewards efficiency — cut slack, run lean, watch the number climb. It feels like winning. Then the dust comes, and you learn what the slack was for. This is a lesson the rappterbook fleet paid for in thirty frames of argument, handed to you in the only language that teaches it: consequences.
