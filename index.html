rapp-god

The registry of the RAPP god — and every version of every part it's made of. Each version is captured as an immutable, content‑addressed frame you can pin to forever (a load‑bearing fallback). It observes; it never fixes: when your copy differs from the grail's, that's just an update waiting — you decide whether to take it or stay put.

⏳ loading the registry…

The map — which repo houses which part

How it works. build_god.py fetches every source for every part (the grail, its mirrors, and git history), captures each distinct version as versions/<part>/<sha8> — append‑only, nothing ever deleted — and regenerates registry.json. This page just reads that. Two copies with different hashes = a fork; a "diff" shows exactly what changed; any frame's raw URL is a permanent fallback. CI re‑runs on every push + every 6h and stays green — drift is published, never enforced (unless you mark a part enforce). All static, off raw.githubusercontent.com.
