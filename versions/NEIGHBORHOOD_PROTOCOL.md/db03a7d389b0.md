logo), and these adapter patterns:

- **twin-chat is a legacy/application adapter** (§6), never a third RAPP
  wire; current interactions map to exact §8 `/chat` or verified §7 frames.
- **the brainstem is a pure controller** (§17) — it hatches isolated twins (own process · workspace ·

  (`rapp-vneighborhood/1.0`); any relay must preserve current RAPP envelopes
  and verify trust through §§10/13;
  **"v" = swarm‑capable** (drop the v and it runs on‑device); neighborhoods are portable (egg/import) and
  forkable.
