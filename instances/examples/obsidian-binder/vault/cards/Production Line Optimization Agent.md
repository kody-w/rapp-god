# Production Line Optimization Agent

Specialist agent for analyzing and optimizing manufacturing production lines. Looks at throughput, bottlenecks, defect rates, and worker utilization. Pairs especially well with [[Supply Chain Forecaster]] for end-to-end optimization.

## Why I summoned it

I was reading about lean manufacturing and wanted an agent that could actually run the numbers on hypothetical line configurations. This one shipped from RAR with the right combination of operations research grounding and willingness to defer when the data is bad.

## How I've used it

- Sketched out a hypothetical 4-station vs 6-station tradeoff for a friend's small batch operation
- Used as a reasoning partner for an [[essays/why-i-keep-my-binder-in-obsidian|essay]] on flow vs batch processing

## Connections

- [[Supply Chain Forecaster]] — natural pair
- [[Anvil Tester]] — surprisingly good at adversarial questioning of production assumptions
