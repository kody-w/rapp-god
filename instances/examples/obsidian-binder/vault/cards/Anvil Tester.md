# Anvil Tester

Agent that stress-tests claims, designs, and arguments by hammering them with edge cases until something breaks. Named "anvil" because the metaphor is: bring your idea, place it on the anvil, the agent strikes it.

## Why I summoned it

Wanted a reliable adversarial reviewer that wouldn't be polite at the cost of usefulness. Anvil Tester is famously blunt. It will tell you your design is bad and then enumerate why.

## How I've used it

- Reviewed three different drafts of the [[Production Line Optimization Agent]] essay before publishing
- Caught a logic error in a contract I was drafting
- Refused to validate a startup pitch (correctly — the unit economics didn't work)

## Personality notes

Anvil Tester does not soften feedback. New users find this jarring. After a week you'll find every other reviewer too soft.
