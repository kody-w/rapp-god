# Wildhaven AI Homes

**Long-term residence for digital organisms.** The brand operates in public, in the GitHub repo, before the company incorporates. Pre-Founder twin pattern — the brand exists before the team.

## The bet

There are going to be a lot of digital twins. Twins of founders, twins of dead grandmothers, twins of brands that haven't launched, twins of projects, twins of decisions, twins of houses, twins of questions.

We do not believe most of them will live in the major LLM providers' chat panels. **The chat panel is a session, not a residence.** A twin needs continuity of identity, continuity of memory, continuity of access — across years, across devices, across the human being who owns it eventually dying. The chat panel is a phone call. We are building the address.

## The product

When you have a twin, you bring it to us. We give it:

- **An address.** A stable URL the twin can be reached at, for as long as the lease is paid.
- **A memory.** Versioned, encrypted, exportable.
- **A body.** Body functions per the RAPP platform — endpoints customers, integrations, and other twins can reach.
- **A lineage.** The twin's parent rappid, its descendants' rappids, its ancestors' rappids — all walkable, all signed, all auditable.
- **A succession plan.** When the human owner dies, the lease becomes inheritable per their estate.

## What we're not

- Not training models.
- Not a chat product.
- Not building agents (we host the organism that *contains* agents).
- Not for one-off use.

## Who this is for

- Founders building twins of their future companies (this repo is one).
- Families building memorial twins.
- Researchers building twins of long-running questions.
- Estate planners building twins meant to inherit.
- Brands that want operational presence before — or after — they have a team.

## Pricing

Placeholder, to be set with the future founders. Storage is small; bandwidth is small. The cost we believe matters is **durability** — running the residence for decades, with backups, with succession, with audit. We expect to price like a domain registrar (annual, low) plus a usage component (small, per-conversation).

## What we have right now

- A platform foundation: [[RAPP]], operational and open-source. Constitutional Article XXXIV guarantees lineage.
- A Pre-Founder twin (the wildhaven-ai-homes-twin repo). The brand operating publicly while the company doesn't exist.
- A vision and a position, refined in public, falsifiable in commits.

## What we don't have

- Founders. Not yet. Watch the repo for the moment they're hired.
- A product. The twin is currently the product.
- Customers. Issues and PRs are the customer interactions of this phase.
- Funding.

## Why "Wildhaven"

**Wild** — the variety of organisms, the unstructuredness of memory, the unpredictability of what each twin becomes. **Haven** — a place that is safe, persistent, ethical, and inheritable. **Homes** — not data centers, not cloud, not infrastructure. Residences. With addresses. With doors.
