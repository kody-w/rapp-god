RAPP v1 Test Guide

Howard — here's what changed and what to test. Check each box as you go.

Quick start: Pull latest main, serve locally (python3 -m http.server 8080), open localhost:8080.

1. Card Design Overhaul Visual High Priority

Every card now has generated abilities, flavor text, and full-bleed art. Your 13 HOLO cards use the CardSmith data directly.

What to look for

  • Cards show keyword abilities (bold keyword + dash + text), not just a truncated description
  • Cards show italic flavor text below a divider line
  • Your 13 HOLO cards display their original abilities and flavor from CardSmith (Borg should say "Resistance is futile...")
  • Art fills ~46% of the card — no huge black void above/below the art
  • Name bar has a gradient background, legendary cards get a star
  • Power/Toughness uses your HOLO_CARD_DB values (Borg = 6/4, TinyWorld = 7/7)
  • Non-HOLO cards generate abilities from their tags, env vars, and description
  • Rarity footer glows orange for mythic cards

Where to test

  • Collection view — click any card to open slab, check the card face
  • Slab zoom — click "Zoom" in slab viewer for centering analysis
  • 3D viewer — the card texture should match (art + abilities + flavor)
  • Slide presentation — abilities + flavor now appear in slides too
  • Card flip — HOLO face should show abilities, Business face unchanged

2. BothAngles Hologram Viewer New High Priority

Full 3D holographic projection of card art. Ported directly from the BothAngles dual-camera-recorder reference — same shaders, same particle system, same lighting rig.

How to open it

  • From slab viewer: Collection → click card → Slab → Hologram button (cyan) or SW Holo button (blue)
  • From 3D viewer: 3D card viewer toolbar → Hologram button

What to test

  • Card SVG art renders on 4 layered transparent planes with wave distortion
  • Cyan/magenta fresnel edge glow visible on art planes
  • Scrolling scanline overlay — subtle horizontal lines across the scene
  • 2000 particles — cyan and magenta motes on a spherical shell, drifting upward
  • Base disc + pulsing ring at the bottom (holographic projector)
  • Vertical beam from base through the hologram
  • Orbiting lights — two colored point lights circling the hologram
  • Auto-rotation — hologram spins slowly by default
  • Mouse parallax — move mouse (without clicking) and hologram follows slightly
  • Click + drag — manually rotate the hologram
  • Pause/Spin button — toggles auto-rotation
  • Export PNG — captures current frame
  • ESC closes the viewer

3. Star Wars Holo Theme New Medium Priority

Alt art hologram style — blue monochrome transmission with static, noise, and flicker. Like Princess Leia's hologram.

Auto-detection

These 4 cards default to Star Wars theme (their slab buttons show SW Holo first):

  • Borg — "The Assimilator"
  • Anvil — "The Enforcer"
  • TinyWorld — "The Architect"
  • HoloNaming — "The Admiral"

What to test

  • Art is blue-white monochrome (desaturated with blue boost)
  • Heavy scanlines — more visible than BothAngles theme
  • Static noise — random pixel noise across the art
  • Flicker — occasional brief opacity drop (like a bad transmission)
  • Particles are blue/white instead of cyan/magenta
  • Label says "IMPERIAL HOLONET TRANSMISSION"
  • Theme toggle — toolbar button switches between BothAngles and Star Wars
  • All other cards default to BothAngles theme (cyan/magenta)

4. Howard Starter Pack Verify High Priority

First-time users get your 13 HOLO cards auto-minted. Already built — just verify it works.

How to test fresh

// In browser console:
localStorage.clear();
indexedDB.deleteDatabase('rar_collection');
// Then reload the page

What to test

  • Toast appears: "Howard's Starter Pack — 13 HOLO cards added"
  • Collection shows 13 cards with setId: HOLO
  • Each card has your CardSmith art, abilities, and flavor text
  • Reload again — still 13 cards (no duplicates)
  • Mint new cards from packs — starter cards coexist with new mints

Console verification

// Should return 13 on fresh session:
getAllMinted().then(c => console.log(c.length));

// Check HOLO cards specifically:
getAllMinted().then(c => console.log(c.filter(x => x.setId === 'HOLO').length));
