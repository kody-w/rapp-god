How a photo becomes an organism

The snapshot is analyzed entirely in your browser — no upload. Its dominant colors become the creature's palette, its brightness its glow, its warmth its shape, and its texture / busyness its spikes and limbs. Those become a hologram-cartridge/1.0 genome — a self-describing, content-addressed organism the universal Lantern renders in 3D. Same photo, same creature; a different photo, a different one. Keep it as an .egg and it lives anywhere.
