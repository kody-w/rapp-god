☎️ The Payphone

A public dial pad for private RAPP doors. Some neighborhoods keep no public front door — on purpose. If you belong inside one, you were handed its rappid (an invite egg, a QR, a message). Dial it with your own GitHub auth. If the door isn't yours to see, the line just rings: 404 — same as a number that doesn't exist.

The payphone is just the handshake: it verifies you can read the door, then hands you into the live room, which runs end-to-end encrypted over WebRTC (DTLS peer-to-peer + the §8 sealed channel keyed by a collaborator-gated secret). Nothing of the live session passes through us or the broker — only ciphertext.
