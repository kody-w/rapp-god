Give it a goal.
Your swarm does the rest.

RAPP is the portable agent layer that accelerates adoption across Microsoft Copilot, Azure, and Microsoft 365. The historical concept explored turning a plain-language goal into a single-file local agent. Azure and Copilot Studio promotion remain retired design goals, not operational tiers.

①

Goal in, swarm out

Describe what you want in natural language. A swarm of agents spins up, coordinates, and learns from the work — so the result improves every time you run it.

②

One file. Travels anywhere.

Every agent is one Python file. Drop it in a Teams chat, paste it in an email, drag it onto a teammate's brainstem — it runs the same wherever it lands.

③

One local source, future adapters

Local source can inform future adapters. No Tier 2, Copilot Studio, or production artifact is currently built or shipped.

Give it your goal. A swarm spins up, swarms around it, and learns from it. A future handoff could carry reviewed source; there is no current install-from-chat or run-immediately contract.
