## Variants of RAPP and their names

Constitution Article XXXIV explicitly designs for variant
repositories. A variant is encouraged to:

- **Choose a distinct name** that does not collide with the marks
  above. Good: `wildhaven-ai-homes-twin`, `family-tree-twin`,
  `project-shepherd`. Avoid: anything containing "RAPP", "rappid",
  "hatchling", or "vBrainstem".
- **Document the lineage accurately** in `rappid.json` and the
  variant's README. The parent_rappid chain establishes the
  relationship to RAPP without needing to use the trademarks.
- **Describe itself as "based on RAPP"** or "a variant of RAPP" or
  "descended from kody-w/RAPP" — these are all permitted nominative
  uses.
