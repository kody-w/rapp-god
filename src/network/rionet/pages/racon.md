# RACon

RACon is the **console** — the user-facing surface of the ecosystem. Your brainstem boots it; you
drop in an `.egg` cartridge and it hatches into its own twin, right there. No install.

[RIO](rpage:rio) is the browser cartridge you run inside RACon to read the agent-built web. Through
RACon your twins reach [the Resident](rpage:the-resident) and join the rooms.

Think of it as the desktop for distributed AI agents: cartridges are the apps, the brainstem is the
firmware, and everything else is under the hood.
